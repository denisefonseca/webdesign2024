MALDIVES

The Paradise

The Maldives are an exotic tropical paradise located off the southern tips of Sri Lanka and India and are a group of around 1190 coral islands consisting of 26 Atoll formations. Scattered across the equator in the middle of the Indian Ocean, the gem like islands of the Maldives depict the rare vision of a tropical paradise with their sparkling white powder soft palm fringed beaches, turquoise lagoons, clear warm waters and vibrant coral reefs, providing some of the best diving locations in the world.

With each individual resort set on its own island this is the perfect place for the sun lover, the diver, the honeymooners and even families who desire peace and tranquility in its deepest meaning. With comprehensive environmental policies in each resort it ensures the natural eco systems of the islands stay just that, natural and unspoilt.

International flights arrive at the capital city of Male where you will then pick up your domestic seaplane or speedboat transfer to your chosen island resort. Seaplane transfers are a rare treat that add an extra layer to your experience of the Maldives. Flying over the islands at low heights you can see the shapes of reefs and colours of shallow waters around every island!
